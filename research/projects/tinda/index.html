TINDA - assess your digital learning competences 

TINDA is an online self-assessment tool for evaluating digital competences and achieved qualification requirements for teachers in all educational levels.

Video description of the tool

Feature list

  • Assess - Tinda enables you to self-assess your digital competences based on the DigCompEdu digital competency model
  • Validate - Tinda enhances you validate your competences based on the evidences
  • Reflect - Tinda supports you to reflect your practices and plan your development

Application fields:

  • Assessment of the teachers' digital competences
  • Participating in EDUlabs initiatives
  • Assessment of and pre-service teachers
  • Integration to the teacher education
  • Policy perspective - Integration to the policy working group of Development of Estonian teachers' digital competences

Use cases

Pre-teacher case

Pre-service teachers are expected to develop digital competences during their studies at the university. Student Kati will pre-test their skills in the beginning of the studies and will get the visualized results to understand the needs for improvement. During the studies, Kati collects evidences about her digital competences to the portfolio in eDidaktikum. After the studies, Kati will be tested again to understand the improvement and Kati can demonstrate the evidences about the progress.

Teacher education case

Teacher education program head Pille is aware of the need to develop the pre-service teachers' digital competences. However, Pille is not aware of the state of the art of students digital competences to plan training for them. Tinda enables her to see the level of digital competences across students groups, which enables her to better plan the curriculum development.

Technical specification

Tinda is developed using PHP content management framework Drupal. The current version of Tinda is running on Drupal 8.6.

It has been customized using following modules developed for Tinda:

  • Questions: To import questions from Tao (a question authoring platform) to Tinda.
  • Qualification Test: To create qualification test.
  • Qualification test round: To create a test round from qualification test, assign it to users, and specify the deadline.
  • Submission: To store the submitted answers for each test round.
  • CSV Downloader: To download data from all submissions in CSV format.
